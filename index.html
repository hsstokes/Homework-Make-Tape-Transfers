Task 2: Make 3 Tape Transfers

1: Texture Transfer

Use your own photo of a texture.

Example: Wood, fabric, leaves

2: Research Image

Use a photo from your research.

Example: Fashion or art image

3: Text Transfer

Use text that connects to your idea.

Example: Quote or special word

How to Make a Tape Transfer:

  1. Put tape over the image
  2. Press hard with a spoon
  3. Put in warm water (10-15 minutes)
  4. Rub paper off gently
  5. Let dry

Task 3: Make Creative Font Choices

Try these ideas with your text:

Font Ideas

  • Try a big or small font size
  • Turn words upside down
  • Mix different fonts
  • Repeat one word many times

Text Placement

  • Put text over your texture photo
  • Put text with your research image
  • Try text in different directions
  • Make text big or small

Note: Write down why you chose each image and text.

Tips for Success

Press tape hard with a card or spoon
Use warm water - not cold
Rub paper off gently
Make extra transfers
Let dry overnight
